What is Travel Buddy?

Travel Buddy is the ultimate guide for your travels. We are here to help you to enjoy your journeys to the maximum and make it as easy as possible for you. Let’s get to know the world together and share your stories in our forum. Stay in touch for daily travel tips and educate your self prior your trip to a new country. Every single time you try and talk to locals in their native language, they will appreaciate it and we are here to teach you the basics. You can also find all the must-see spots in one place so it is way easier to add it to your bucket list. You can also find a bunch of tips for what food or drinks to try in each country so you do not miss out on the national traditional delicious treats.
