Czech Republic

The Czech Republic, also known as Czechia or historically known as Bohemia, is a landlocked country in Central Europe. The Czech Republic has a hilly landscape that covers an area of 78,871 square kilometers (30,452 sq mi) with a mostly temperate continental and oceanic climate. The capital and largest city is Prague; other major cities and urban areas include Brno, Ostrava, Plzeň and Liberec.
